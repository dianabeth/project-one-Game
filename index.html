HELP, STOP THE SPREAD!!!

Use ← ↓ ↑ → on your keyboard to find your way around!!!

  • Stop the virus from spreading by keeping areas around you squeaky clean.
  • The use of protective gear gives a boost to your health.
  • Spraying on protective gear with disinfectants will result in a decrease in health.
  • Coming in contact with the virus can result in an infection!!!
  • Hint: Repeatedly hitting on the spacebar will disinfect areas above you...watch out for the protective gear though!!!
